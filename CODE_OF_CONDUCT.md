# 🤝 Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

### 👍 Acceptable Behavior

| Behavior | Examples |
|:---------|:---------|
| **Respect** | Using welcoming and inclusive language |
| **Collaboration** | Being open to feedback and working together |
| **Acceptance** | Accepting responsibility for mistakes |
| **Support** | Focusing on what's best for the community |
| **Openness** | Being open to different viewpoints and experiences |

### 👎 Unacceptable Behavior

| Behavior | Examples |
|:---------|:---------|
| **Harassment** | Violence, threats, or intimidation |
| **Discrimination** | Racist, sexist, or discriminatory language |
| **Invasion** | Deliberate intimidation, stalking, following |
| **Privacy** | Publishing private information without consent |
| **Disruption** | Deliberately disrupting conversations or events |

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

### Who Enforces

| Role | Responsibility |
|:-----|:---------------|
| **Community Leaders** | Enforce standards of acceptable behavior |
| **Maintainers** | Review and address conduct violations |
| **Project Owner** | Final authority on enforcement decisions |

### Enforcement Powers

| Power | Use Case |
|:------|:---------|
| **Warning** | Minor violations, first offense |
| **Temporary Ban** | Serious violations, repeated issues |
| **Permanent Ban** | Severe violations, pattern of abuse |

## Enforcement Guidelines

### Violation Levels

#### 1️⃣ Minor Incidents

| Aspect | Description |
|:-------|:------------|
| **Examples** | Inappropriate language, ignoring others |
| **Response** | Private warning, public acknowledgment |
| **Consequence** | Reminder of community standards |

#### 2️⃣ Serious Incidents

| Aspect | Description |
|:-------|:------------|
| **Examples** | Continued minor issues, single serious violation |
| **Response** | 24-hour temporary ban |
| **Consequence** | No participation in community spaces |

#### 3️⃣ Severe Incidents

| Aspect | Description |
|:-------|:------------|
| **Examples** | Physical threats, harassment, privacy violations |
| **Response** | Immediate permanent ban |
| **Consequence** | Complete removal from community |

## Reporting Guidelines

### How to Report

| Method | When to Use |
|:-------|:------------|
| **GitHub Issues** | Non-urgent violations |
| **Direct Message** | Sensitive issues |
| **Email** | Private concerns |

### What to Include

```markdown
## Report Details

**Who**: [Person(s) involved]

**What**: [What happened]

**When**: [Date and time]

**Where**: [Location/platform]

**Evidence**: [Screenshots, links, etc.]
```

### Reporting Response Timeline

| Priority | Description | Response Time |
|:---------|:------------|:--------------|
| 🔴 Urgent | Safety concerns | Within 24 hours |
| 🟠 High | Serious violations | Within 48 hours |
| 🟡 Medium | General issues | Within 1 week |
